    Overview

    • Product name

      Folic acid (Folate), Synthetic vitamin B9

    • Description

      Synthetic vitamin B9. Tetrahydrofolate precursor.

    • Biological description

      Synthetic vitamin B9. Tetrahydrofolate precursor. Promotes DNA synthesis and cell division. Shows anti-inflammatory and chemopreventive effects in vivo. Orally active.

    • Purity

      > 96%

    • CAS Number

      59-30-3

    • Chemical structure

    Properties

    • Chemical name

      (2S)-2-[[4-[(2-Amino-4-oxo-1H-pteridin-6-yl)methylamino]benzoyl]amino]pentanedioic acid

    • Molecular weight

      441.40

    • Molecular formula

      C19H19N7O6

    • PubChem identifier

      6037

    • Storage instructions

      Store at +4°C. The product can be stored for up to 12 months.

    • Solubility overview

      Soluble in 1 N NaOH to 100 mM

    • Handling

      Wherever possible, you should prepare and use solutions on the same day. However, if you need to make up stock solutions in advance, we recommend that you store the solution as aliquots in tightly sealed vials at -20°C. Generally, these will be useable for up to one month. Before use, and prior to opening the vial we recommend that you allow your product to equilibrate to room temperature for at least 1 hour.

      Need more advice on solubility, usage and handling? Please visit our frequently asked questions (FAQ) page for more details.
